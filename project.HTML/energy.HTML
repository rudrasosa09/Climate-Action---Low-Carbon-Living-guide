Quick fact

Switching to 5‑star appliances can cut energy usage by 30–50%. Over years, this saves money and reduces your carbon footprint.

Why choose efficient appliances?

Lower bills, reduced power usage, longer lifespan, and eco‑friendly operation. Ideal for homes aiming for sustainability.

Common energy‑efficient appliances

  • LED lights — 80% less energy than incandescent
  • Inverter AC — adjusts speed for better efficiency
  • Front‑load washing machines — use less water and power
  • Refrigerators with inverter compressors — lower running cost
  • Induction cooktops — faster and more efficient heating

Smart usage tips

  • Choose appliances with higher star ratings.
  • Unplug devices when not in use to avoid phantom load.
  • Use eco‑modes in washing machines and AC units.
  • Regular maintenance improves efficiency.
